FAQs

How it works

How do I download the app?

To download the Scoot app, you can search “Scoot” in both the App and Google Play stores. An even simpler way to do it would be to click the relevant link at the bottom of this page and you’ll be re-directed to the correct page.

Can I find a nearby Scoots?

Yes, you can! Just download the Scoot app and locate the nearest scooter available. It's fast, easy, and fun!

Do I need a license to ride?

Do I need a license to ride? It depends on what you want to ride. For bicycles, you don't need a license. For motorcycles, you need a separate license. For scooters and mopeds, it varies by state.
